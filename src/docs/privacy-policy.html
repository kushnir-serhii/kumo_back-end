Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology to power its chat feature. Your chat messages are processed by OpenAI to generate responses. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details on how your data is handled during AI processing.


The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.


For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information collected and retained includes:

  • Subscription information: purchase token, next payment date

The information that the Service Provider requests will be retained by them and used as described in this privacy policy.
